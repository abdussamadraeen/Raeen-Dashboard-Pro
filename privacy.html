Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2026

Raeen Dashboard Pro ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Raeen Dashboard Pro **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data. All your settings, shortcuts, and notes are stored locally on your device using your browser's storage (LocalStorage and IndexedDB).

2. Data Transmission

The Extension does not send your data to any external servers. Any interactions with search engines (like Google or Bing) occur directly between your browser and those services, as if you were visiting them directly.

3. Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions for functional reasons only:

  • Storage: To save your dashboard settings and notes locally.
  • DeclarativeNetRequest: To allow the integration of search engines within the dashboard frames.
  • TopSites: To display your most visited websites if you enable that feature.

4. Third-Party Services

When using the integrated search dashboards (Google, Bing), you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective services. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
