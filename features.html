Integrated AI Engines

Access the world's most powerful AI models directly from your search bar. Switch between ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity in a single click without ever leaving your tab.

Our smart-routing technology ensures zero-redirect latency, keeping your focus where it belongs.

Zero-Flash Preloader

Standard dashboards often suffer from a jarring "black flash" when opening a new tab. Raeen Pro uses an aggressive, low-level preloader that initializes state before the first frame is rendered.

The result is a smooth, instant transition from browser to dashboard, every single time.
